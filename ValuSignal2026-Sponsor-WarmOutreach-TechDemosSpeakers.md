## DESIGN NOTES

**Why warm outreach is structured differently than cold:**
- No need to explain what ValuSignal is — they already said yes to it
- Opening line leads with *their* existing commitment, not the event pitch
- The ask is framed as "extend what you're already doing," not "come join something"
- Shorter than cold emails — context is shared, less setup needed

**Key framing decisions:**
- Variant A: "November 2 on their calendar" — ties directly to why the demo audience is motivated, makes the ROI immediate
- Variant A: "not just those who join your session" — the contrast that makes the sponsorship value land
- Variant B: uses {{company}} token so the email feels personal to both company-backed and independent speakers
- Variant B: "sponsorship is how that works" — natural close, removes friction without explaining tiers upfront

**Neighbor test:** Both pass. Read either one out loud — it sounds like a conversation, not a campaign.
