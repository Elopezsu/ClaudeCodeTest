## SECTION 3: SPEAKER PROFILE — 2:30–5:50
*[Click into Speaker Profile in the left nav]*

"Click **Speaker Profile** in the left sidebar. This is where you build out your public presence for ValuSignal. Let me walk you through each section."

### 3a. Profile Photo — 2:30–3:00

"First up is your **profile photo** — this is the most important item, and it's currently the top pending task for most speakers.

Click 'Upload Photo' and upload a JPG or PNG that is at least **800 by 800 pixels**. A few tips: use a professional headshot, pick a solid or simple background, make sure your face is well-lit and clearly visible, and a square crop works best. This photo appears on your speaker card on the ValuSignal website, so make it a good one."

### 3b. Basic Info — 3:00–3:20

"Scrolling down to **Basic Info** — your name, title, company, and phone number were pre-filled from your invitation, so just review these and correct anything if needed."

### 3c. Credentials & Designations — 3:20–3:50

"Next is **Credentials and Designations**. Select your **Appraiser Credential Level** — Certified Residential, Licensed Residential, Certified General, Aspiring Appraiser, or Other. This helps us position your speaker card correctly for the audience.

Then select all **Professional Designations** that apply — MAI, SRA, ASA, AI-GRS, CDEI, and many more. You can select multiple. Add any board memberships or organization roles in the field below — those appear under your name on the speaker card."

### 3d. Speaker Bio — 3:50–4:20

"The **Speaker Bio** is where you tell your story. We recommend **150 to 300 words**. Write in third person, lead with your designation and current role, and include notable achievements or areas of expertise. This is what attendees read before deciding to join your session."

### 3e. Web Presence — 4:20–4:45

"In **Web Presence**, add your LinkedIn URL, personal or firm website, employer website, blog, or any other platform — podcast, YouTube, course platform. If you have nothing to share, just check the 'None' box."

### 3f. Logos & Branding — 4:45–5:05

"Under **Logos and Branding**, upload your primary company logo — transparent PNG preferred, minimum 400 pixels wide, max 5 MB. A secondary logo is optional. If you don't have one to provide, check the box that says so."

### 3g. Permissions & Preferences — 5:05–5:25

"**Permissions and Preferences** has two quick questions: can we feature your information on the ValuSignal website alongside your session? And would you like to submit a quote for the site — a short thought on valuation or why you're excited about the event? Quick to answer, and we appreciate it."

### 3h. Profile Outbound Link — 5:25–5:50

"The **Profile Outbound Link** — your speaker profile on the ValuSignal website will have a single prominent 'Learn More' or 'Visit' button. You choose where it sends visitors: personal website, employer website, LinkedIn, blog, or another URL. Pick whichever destination is most professionally valuable to you."

## SECTION 4: YOUR AFFILIATE PROGRAM — 5:50–7:30
*[Scroll down to Section 8 on the Speaker Profile page]*

"Now I want to make sure we spend real time on this next section — it is one of the most valuable things inside your portal, and we don't want you to skip over it.

Scrolling down, you'll find **Section 8: Your Affiliate Program**. Please read every word here carefully — there is real money on the table, and all you have to do is share a link.

**Here's how it works:** as a ValuSignal speaker, you have a unique affiliate link tied to your account. Every time someone buys a ticket by clicking your link, you earn **$47 automatically**. No invoicing, no chasing payments, no manual work. The platform behind this is called **Rewardful** — it handles everything at checkout and logs the commission to your account the moment a purchase is made.

**Your affiliate link is yours to use anywhere.** Drop it in an email to your clients. Post it on LinkedIn. Mention it on a podcast. Put it in your email signature. Share it in a newsletter. Think about how many appraisers, assessors, or valuation-adjacent professionals are in your network — every one of them is a potential $47.

**On tracking:** Rewardful uses a first-party browser cookie with a **60-day referral window**. If someone clicks your link but doesn't buy right away, as long as they come back and purchase within 60 days the commission still goes to you. And because it uses first-party cookies, it won't be blocked by most privacy settings or ad blockers.

**Your Rewardful dashboard** will show you: Visitors — people who clicked your link; Conversions — completed purchases credited to you; and Earnings — your running commission balance.

**Getting paid:** commissions are paid on the **5th of each month** via Wise bank transfer, covering all commissions from the prior calendar month. Your Rewardful account details must be completed by the **3rd of that month** to be included. Miss the 3rd and your commissions are held and paid the following month — they never expire, so you won't lose anything, but don't delay.

**Now — here's how to set up your Rewardful account.** The portal walks you through it in five steps:

- **Step 1 — Access the platform:** click the direct Rewardful button in your profile, or go directly to valusignal.getrewardful.com/login.
- **Step 2 — Log in:** use the unique username and password provided right here in your portal — scroll down to 'Your Rewardful Credentials' and you'll see them ready to copy.
- **Step 3 — Set up payouts:** add your Wise bank transfer details so you actually receive your commissions when they're paid out.
- **Step 4 — Get your link:** copy your unique affiliate link directly from your Rewardful dashboard.
- **Step 5 — Start sharing and tracking your referrals.**

Do this today. Your credentials are already in the portal waiting for you — this is passive income you've already earned by agreeing to speak. Don't leave it sitting there unclaimed."

## SECTION 7: DISCLOSURES & AGREEMENTS — 8:20–9:00
*[Click Disclosures in the left nav]*

"This next section is very important — please don't skip it. Click **Disclosures** in the left sidebar.

Here you'll find three documents that outline the terms of your participation as a ValuSignal 2026 speaker. **Your profile does not go live until the signature documents are completed**, so make sure you handle these promptly.

**Document 1 — Media Release & Photo/Video Consent.** This one requires your electronic signature. By signing, you grant ValuSignal permission to photograph and record your participation, and to use your name, image, voice, and biographical information for promotional and educational purposes — including on the conference website, social media, marketing materials, post-event recap videos, and educational content. You're also authorizing us to use your submitted headshot, company logo, and bio in the conference program and app. This release is valid in perpetuity unless revoked in writing. Read it in full, check the box that you've read and agree, and click **Sign & Acknowledge**.

**Document 2 — Speaker Agreement & Terms of Participation.** This also requires your signature. It covers your presentation commitment — delivering the session as described in your accepted proposal with original, accurate, and appropriate content. It covers content guidelines: your presentation must be educational and free from overt commercial promotion, and you agree to disclose any conflicts of interest related to your topic. It also covers intellectual property — you retain full ownership of your content, but you grant ValuSignal a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to reproduce and distribute it for conference-related purposes, including post-event distribution to registered attendees. And it covers compliance with applicable laws and professional standards, including USPAP where relevant. Read it fully and sign.

**Document 3 — Event Code of Conduct.** This one is review-only — no signature needed. It contains the full event guide and policies for ValuSignal 2026, covering the virtual format, presentation logistics, technical requirements, the materials deadline, and our code of conduct. Once you've read it, click **Mark as Reviewed**.

Note: signature documents require your electronic acknowledgment before your profile goes live. If you have any questions about any of these, contact us at speakers@valusignal.com."
