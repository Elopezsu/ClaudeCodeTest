## ✉️ Outreach copy — the referral-partner ask

Keep it short, warm, peer-to-peer. These are already booked speakers, so lead with *"you're already in"* and make the ask feel like an easy add-on. Personalize the **[hook]** line for each person (see hooks below) — that one specific detail is what makes it land.

### Subject line options
- A quick idea for you, [First Name]
- Want a referral link for ValuSignal?
- [First Name] — turn your audience into a thank-you

### Base message (email or LinkedIn DM)

> Hi [First Name],
>
> So glad to have you speaking at ValuSignal 2026. [hook — one specific, genuine line about their audience/work.]
>
> That's actually why I'm reaching out: we've opened a **referral program** for our speakers, and I think you'd be a natural fit. It's simple — I send you a **unique referral link**, and you earn **[commission / reward — e.g. $X or X% per ticket]** on anyone who registers through it. No selling, just share it the way you already talk to your audience.
>
> Want me to set you up with a link? Happy to send it over today.
>
> Thanks either way — see you in September!
> Eunice
> eunice@valusignal.com · +1 469 501 1207

### Even shorter (DM / text version)

> Hi [First Name] — loved getting you on the ValuSignal 2026 lineup. Quick one: we've opened a **speaker referral program** — you share a unique link, earn **[reward]** per registration. Given [hook], you'd be a perfect fit. Want me to send your link?
