### Metric definitions
- **Positive reply rate** = Opportunities ÷ Contacts. The "real" rate — use as the headline.
- **Positive share of replies** = Opportunities ÷ all replies. Quality of replies; Yurocket target ≥25–30%.
- **All-reply rate** (what Instantly shows by default) = all replies ÷ contacts. Keep only as a **deliverability signal** — when it drops, it can mean landing in spam.
- Note: in the weekly PDF the campaign rows use *emails sent* as the denominator while the headline 1.83% uses *new contacts (1,970)*; cumulative figures use *contacts/sequence-started*, matching how Instantly computes reply rate.
