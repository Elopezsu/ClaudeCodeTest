### Category line (tagline)

### Headline
From Order Intake to UAD 3.6 Export

### Description
Appraisal Inbox helps independent appraisers, teams, and multi-office firms track orders, coordinate assignments, and manage the entire workflow in one place — with faster intake through Portal Push and Order Forward, and exports including UAD 3.6 MISMO .zip to reduce double entry.

### Features (title + supporting line)
**Faster Order Intake**
Capture orders automatically with Portal Push for lender/AMC portals and Order Forward for order emails.

**Assignment Workflow**
Track status, due dates, team roles, tasklists, workfiles, and messages in a single order record.

**Business Visibility**
See fees, invoices, payments, commissions, workload, and turn times across your whole operation.

**UAD 3.6 Export Handoff**
Export flexibly, including UAD 3.6 MISMO .zip and QuickBooks, to cut duplicate entry downstream.
