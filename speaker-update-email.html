The questions we hear most

Is my session live or pre-recorded?

Most sessions are simulive — you pre-record with me ahead of time, and it plays to the audience at your scheduled slot as if it were live, while you join the chat to answer questions in real time. Lower stress, no tech risk on the day.

How does it run inside Zoom Events?

You won't be doing this alone. I'll be with you for the recording, and I'll send you simple prep instructions beforehand so you're ready. The event is multitrack, all hosted in Zoom Events.

Where are the deck templates?

Branded deck templates and backgrounds are in the media kit inside the speaker portal — use them so we keep everyone's slides visually aligned.
