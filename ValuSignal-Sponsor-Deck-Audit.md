## What Was Overpromised in the Decks
*Source: Tyler Copeland (Zoom) demo call transcript, Jan 9, 2026*

| Claim in Deck | What Tyler Actually Said | Fix |
|---------------|--------------------------|-----|
| "Up to 20 live reps" (Title booth) | **"They can have four concurrent meetings if they have four representatives."** Platinum gets up to 4 concurrent meeting rooms — not 20 reps. | Change to "up to 4 live meeting rooms in your booth" |
| "Up to 10 live reps" (Featured vs Title table) | Same — no 10-rep tier exists. Gold/standard gets fewer meeting rooms than Platinum. | Remove the rep count table entirely. Describe as "meeting rooms" not "reps" |
| "Meeting Scheduler — Title Exclusive" | Meeting rooms exist in Platinum booths. Not confirmed as exclusive to Title vs Featured. | Remove "Title Exclusive" label. Say "booth meeting rooms included" |
| "Co-branding across all session pages" | **Tyler was blunt: "I don't think you're going to love what you see"** — session-level sponsor branding is very limited. Can add a text box with a link but cannot embed logos or images in sessions natively. | Remove "co-branding across sessions." Limit the promise to: lobby page, emails, event page, and recordings intro card (manual). |
| "Session Bumpers in all recordings" | Not a native Zoom feature. Tyler confirmed no native session-level branding. | Only promise this if you will manually add a co-branded intro card before publishing replays. |
| "CRM populated in real time" | **Tyler confirmed: Pipedrive integration requires API or Zapier — "technical expertise may be required."** It is NOT native Zoom. | Change to: "leads delivered as CSV post-event. CRM sync available via Zapier (requires setup)." |
| "Full Analytics Manifest" with specific fields | Analytics section exists but Tyler never confirmed podcast viewership, per-email CTR, or per-session drop-off as standard outputs. | Say "post-event analytics report" — do not list specific fields you haven't seen in the platform. |
| "30 days post-event homepage placement" | This is a manual website task — not a Zoom feature. Deliverable, but your team must maintain it. | Keep the promise — just make sure it's in your onboarding checklist as a manual task. |
| Social media integration | **Tyler explicitly said: "No built-in integration available."** Separate ads must be created manually. | Do not imply social amplification is automated. Say "sponsor acknowledgment posts" (manual). |

## Corrected Copy — By Tier

### CORE PARTNER — $1,000 · 20 slots

**What's real and deliverable:**
- Logo on ValuSignal.com, linked to your site, from signing through the event
- Your listing in the Zoom Events Expo Hall — visible to every registered attendee
- Your logo in every attendee-facing email (pre-event, event day, post-event recap)
- Sponsor acknowledgment posts on ValuSignal social channels (posted manually by ValuSignal Marketing Manager — not a Zoom native feature)

**Suggested slide copy (replaces current):**

> **Four placements. One industry. $1,000.**
>
> 🌐 **Website Listing** — Your logo on ValuSignal.com, linked to your site, live from signing through post-event.
>
> 🏛 **Expo Hall Listing** — Your brand in the Zoom Events lobby, visible to every attendee who opens the event.
>
> ✉️ **Email Visibility** — Your logo in every email we send to attendees before, during, and after the event.
>
> 📢 **Social Recognition** — Sponsor shout-out posts on ValuSignal's social channels, published by our Marketing Manager during the campaign.

*No changes needed to ROI math slide ($0.50/impression) — that math is sound.*

### FEATURED PARTNER — $2,500 · 10 slots

**What's real and deliverable:**
- Everything in Core
- Virtual Expo Hall booth — your company video, downloadable content (up to 3 images, up to 5 PDFs), and a live rep in your booth
- Auto lead capture — every booth visitor logged with name, email, and organization
- Post-event attendee list delivered as CSV within 5 business days
- 5-minute speaking slot (live or pre-recorded simulive) during a main session
- One dedicated email to the full attendee list — your subject line, your copy, your CTA

**What to remove or correct:**
- Remove "real-time CRM population" → replace with "leads delivered as CSV post-event. CRM sync available via Zapier (requires setup)."
- Remove any specific rep count claims — Featured booth supports standard rep access; do not promise a number until confirmed with Zoom for this tier
- Remove any session-level co-branding claims — Tyler confirmed session pages have very limited sponsor visibility
- Social posts: keep as a deliverable — ValuSignal Marketing Manager posts manually. Do not imply Zoom automation.

**Suggested slide copy (replaces "Five Tools. One Integrated Pipeline"):**

> **Five things you can't get from a logo placement.**
>
> 💻 **Virtual Booth** — Your video, your downloads, your rep live in the booth. Every visitor automatically logged with name, email, and organization.
>
> 📋 **Attendee List** — Full export of every registered attendee. Delivered as a CSV within 5 business days of the event.
>
> 🎤 **5-Minute Speaking Slot** — Your message, your slides, in front of the full audience during a main session. Live or pre-recorded, your choice.
>
> ✉️ **Dedicated Email** — One email sent by ValuSignal under your brand. Your subject line, your body, your CTA.
>
> ✅ **All Core Placements** — Website, Expo Hall, all event emails, social recognition included.

**Suggested corrected "hard questions" answers:**

> *"$2,500 is too much for an unproven event."*
> In Year 2, a booth, an attendee list, a speaking slot, and a dedicated email to 300–600 warm valuation professionals won't be available for $2,500. You're getting pioneer pricing because the event is new.

> *"What if the lead list is small?"*
> Our projection is 300–600 attendees based on association partner reach. The lead list is every registered attendee — the number is real and auditable.

### TITLE SPONSOR — $10,000 · 1 slot only

**What's real and deliverable:**
- Everything in Featured
- "Presented by [Your Brand]" co-branding on the website, every attendee email, registration confirmation, and social posts
- Co-branded intro card on all session recordings — manually added by ValuSignal before replays are published (this is a ValuSignal team commitment, not a native Zoom feature)
- "Powered by" above-the-fold placement on ValuSignal.com homepage, live from signing through 30 days post-event
- Podcast interview — 10–15 minutes with an industry host, your choice of live on stage during the event or pre-recorded and aired as a session
- Premium Expo Hall booth — priority placement (Platinum tier), your video, downloadable content (up to 5 images, up to 5 PDFs), live rep chat, and up to 4 concurrent meeting rooms in your booth
- Full post-event analytics report (booth visits, email performance, session data)
- Complete attendee lead list as CSV plus in-booth lead capture — delivered post-event

**What to remove or correct:**
- Remove "up to 20 live reps" → Tyler confirmed: "They can have four concurrent meetings if they have four representatives." Say "up to 4 concurrent meeting rooms in your booth."
- Remove "CRM populated in real time" → leads delivered as CSV post-event. Zapier integration available for CRM sync (requires technical setup).
- Remove "Full Analytics Manifest" as a branded term → say "post-event analytics report"
- Remove "co-branding across all session pages" → Tyler said: "I don't think you're going to love what you see" — session-level branding is very limited natively. Only promise what ValuSignal controls manually: the intro card on recordings.
- Remove "Meeting Scheduler — Title Exclusive" label → meeting rooms exist in Platinum booths but exclusivity to Title was not confirmed by Zoom
- Social recognition → manual posts by ValuSignal Marketing Manager, not automated or integrated with Zoom. Keep as a deliverable — it's real, just not a Zoom feature.

**Suggested slide copy (replaces "Every Tool. Every Touchpoint. Every Attendee."):**

> **Everything we can offer. Given to one company.**
>
> 🏷 **"Presented by" Co-Branding** — Your brand alongside ValuSignal on the website, every attendee email, registration confirmation, social posts, and as a co-branded intro card on all session recordings.
>
> ⭐ **"Powered By" Homepage** — Above the fold on ValuSignal.com from signing through 30 days post-event. Every visitor sees your brand first.
>
> 🎙 **Podcast Interview** — 10–15 minutes with an industry host, your choice: live on stage in front of the full audience, or pre-recorded and aired as a featured session. Your story, your company, your format.
>
> 💻 **Premium Expo Hall Booth** — Priority placement, company video, downloadable content, live rep chat, and up to 4 concurrent meeting rooms so your team can talk to multiple attendees at the same time.
>
> 📋 **Complete Lead List** — Full attendee export plus in-booth lead capture. Every name, email, and organization — delivered as a CSV after the event.
>
> 📊 **Post-Event Analytics** — Booth visits, email performance, and session data delivered to your team after the event.
>
> 📢 **Social Recognition** — Dedicated sponsor posts on ValuSignal's social channels, published by our Marketing Manager throughout the campaign.

**Suggested corrected "Five Questions" answers:**

> *"It's too expensive."*
> $10,000 for co-branding across every touchpoint, a podcast interview (live or pre-recorded — your choice), priority booth placement with 4 meeting rooms, the full attendee list, and an analytics report. What does it cost your team to build that independently? Run the comparison.

> *"There's no track record."*
> There's no track record because this has never been done. Three national valuation associations are behind this event. Being early isn't the risk — missing the founding window is.

> *"I need board approval."*
> I can send a one-page summary with the deliverables, the price, and the scarcity case. One slot. Do you want that today?
