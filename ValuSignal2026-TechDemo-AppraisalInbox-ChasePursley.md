## Platform overview
Manages the business workflow around appraisal assignments: order intake, scheduling, assignments, tasklists, client requirements, workfiles, communication, accounting, exports, and reporting. Serves independent appraisers, appraisal practices, production/office managers, and enterprise/multi-office firms.

**Demo focus (4 pillars):**
1. **Faster intake** — Portal Push (browser plugin for lender/AMC portals) + Order Forward (order emails).
2. **Assignment workflow** — status, due dates, team roles, tasklists, workfiles, messages, activity history in one order record.
3. **Business visibility** — fees, invoices, payments, commissions, workload, turn times, performance.
4. **Export handoff** — flexible exports including **UAD 3.6 MISMO `.zip`** and QuickBooks invoice export, to reduce duplicate entry.
