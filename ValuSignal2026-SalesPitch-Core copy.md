**"We don't know how many people will actually show up."**
> "Our projection is 300–600 based on three national association partners. We'll give you the real attendance number post-event alongside your placement confirmation. The floor is well-sourced."

**"We're not sure this audience is our buyer."**
> "The ValuSignal audience is 40% independent appraisers, 25% appraisal firm owners, 20% lenders and AMC professionals, and 15% tech and service providers. If your product or service touches the valuation workflow, these are your buyers."

**"We need to think about it."**
> "Totally fair. I'll send you a one-pager. Just know there are 20 slots and we have active conversations going — I'd hate for you to miss the founding window."
