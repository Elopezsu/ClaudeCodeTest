   Register here: https://www.valusignal.com/register

→ ValuSignal 2026 is non-CE by design. That means speakers can go much deeper
   on the topics that matter — practical strategy, UAD 3.6, AI tools — without
   the constraints of a CE format.

If they ask about UAD 3.6:
→ Yes, UAD 3.6 readiness is one of the core sessions. The conference is
   September 18–19, which is before the November 2 implementation date,
   so attendees will have time to apply what they learn.

→ For teams of 10+ appraisers, email info@valusignal.com for group rates.

If they ask about speaking or sponsoring:
→ Direct them to https://www.valusignal.com/sponsor

→ "No problem at all, I'll take you off the list. Thanks for letting me know."
   Then mark them as unsubscribed in Instantly.ai.
