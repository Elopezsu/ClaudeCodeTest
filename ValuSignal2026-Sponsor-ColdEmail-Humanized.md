## HUMANIZATION NOTES

Key changes from v1 draft:

| Before | After | Why |
|---|---|---|
| "built exclusively" | "built just" (Variant A) | One word, same meaning, more conversational |
| "no adjacent industries in the room" | "no other industries in the room" | Removes jargon; same contrast |
| "Three sponsor tiers cover the range from brand visibility to a live virtual booth with lead capture, a speaking slot, and full event co-branding" | "We have three sponsor options — from getting your logo in front of every attendee to running a live booth, taking a speaking slot, and having your name on the event" | Removes 4 jargon terms at once |

| "one exclusive Title Sponsor slot" | "one Title Sponsor slot" | "one slot" already means exclusive — redundant |
| "Partners include / are committed partners" | "are on board" | Consistent natural phrasing across both variants |
