# ValuSignal 2026 — Sponsor Closing CTA (standard block)

Reusable closing CTA for sponsor outreach (email, deck closing slide, LinkedIn). Pairs a one-pager hand-off with a primary ask and a gracious referral fallback. Keep it short.

---

## ✅ Final version (recommended)

> **We've put together a one-pager with all the details — [attached / below].**
>
> **Ready to make an impact?** We'd love to have you involved in ValuSignal 2026 — just reach out to **eunice@valusignal.com**.
>
> Not the right time? No problem — a quick intro or a recommendation of someone who'd be a strong fit would mean a lot.

## Compact one-liner version

> **Attached is a one-pager with the full details.** Ready to make an impact? Reach out to **eunice@valusignal.com** and let's talk. And if now's not the time, a quick intro or referral to a potential sponsor would mean a lot.

## One-pager line — phrasing by format
- **Email attachment:** "We've attached a one-pager with all the details."
- **In-message (below):** "We've put together a one-pager with all the details below."
- **Linked:** "Here's a one-pager with everything you need to know: [link]."

## Tone variants for the ask line
- **Partnership/warm:** "Ready to make an impact? We'd love to have you involved in ValuSignal 2026 — just reach out to eunice@valusignal.com."
- **Punchy:** "Want to make an impact at ValuSignal 2026? Email eunice@valusignal.com and let's make it happen."

---

### Notes
- Lead with the one-pager so the reader has the "more info" reassurance **before** the ask — the CTA then feels like a natural next step.
- Bold the email address so the action is unmissable.
- Referral framed as a genuine favor ("would mean a lot"), not a brush-off — keeps the door open and makes recommending easy (one combined ask: intro *or* referral).
- Contact: **eunice@valusignal.com**
