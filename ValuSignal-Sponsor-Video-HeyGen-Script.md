# ValuSignal 2026 — Sponsor Packages Video (HeyGen production script)

**Goal:** ~60-second talking-head video promoting the three sponsorship tiers.
**Use:** embed in the speaker/sponsor outreach email + LinkedIn + share link.

## Recommended HeyGen setup
- **Aspect ratio:** 16:9 (landscape) — best for email embed + LinkedIn feed. *(Make a 9:16 vertical cut too if you'll post to Reels/Shorts.)*
- **Avatar:** a professional, warm business avatar (or your own HeyGen avatar if you've recorded one — a real face converts better for outreach).
- **Voice:** clear US English, warm and confident, **speed ~0.95–1.0** (the script is tight; don't rush).
- **Background:** clean office or brand-navy (#223042) solid; add the ValuSignal logo top-corner.
- **On-screen text:** show the tier name + price as lower-thirds while each tier is spoken (viewers watch muted).
- **End card:** logo + "eunice@valusignal.com" + "Sept 18–19, 2026."

> ⏱️ **Timing note:** the full original script is ~135 words ≈ 57–60s at normal pace — right at the ceiling. The **trimmed version below (~120 words)** gives you breathing room to stay under 60s. Use the trimmed one unless you want it wall-to-wall.
