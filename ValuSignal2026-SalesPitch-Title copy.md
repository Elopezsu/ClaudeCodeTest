## OBJECTIONS & RESPONSES

**"$10,000 is a lot."**
> "Let's run the comparison. A podcast interview with a warm industry audience, co-branding across every touchpoint for months, a premium booth with 4 meeting rooms, the full attendee list, a dedicated email, and a post-event analytics report. What does your team spend to build that kind of reach and credibility in this specific market independently? This is a bundled, exclusive position — and it's only available once."

**"There's no track record."**
> "You're right — there's no track record because this is the founding event. Three national valuation associations are behind it. The audience is built-in. Being the Title Sponsor of the first ValuSignal conference is a different story to tell than being a sponsor of the fifth. The founding window doesn't come back."

**"I need to get board approval."**
> "I can send you a one-page summary: four headline deliverables, the price, the payment structure — 50% now, 50% by August — and the fact that there is one slot. Do you want that today so you have something concrete to take in?"

**"Can you hold the slot while we decide?"**
> "I can give you 48 hours with a verbal. The slot isn't formally held until the deposit, but I won't bring it to anyone else in that window. After that I have to keep the conversation open."

**"What's the podcast interview exactly?"**
> "It's a hosted conversation — 10 to 15 minutes — with one of our industry hosts. You're not presenting slides. You're talking about your company, your perspective on the valuation market, what you're building. It's authentic and it plays well with this audience. You choose whether it's live during the event or pre-recorded ahead of time — either way the full audience sees it."

**"How do the meeting rooms work in the booth?"**
> "Inside your Expo Hall booth, attendees can request a live meeting with one of your reps. You can have up to 4 of those happening at the same time — so if four attendees want to talk to someone from your team simultaneously, that's fully supported. Every booth visitor is also automatically logged whether they join a meeting or not."

**"We want the leads to go straight to our CRM."**
> "The lead list comes as a CSV post-event, delivered within 5 business days. If you want it auto-syncing to your CRM, that's available via Zapier — your team sets it up on your end. We can point you to the documentation."
