Your Session Format
What is a pre-recorded "simulive" session?
Simulive means "simulated live." You record your presentation with us ahead of time, and it plays to the audience at your scheduled slot exactly as if it were happening live. To attendees it feels like a live session — but the video is already polished and locked in, so there's zero risk of a dropped connection, a screen-share glitch, or a slide that won't advance on the big day.
Do I still interact with the audience?
Yes — that's the best part. While your recording plays, you join the session chat and answer questions in real time. You get the polish of a recording and the connection of a live Q&A, without having to present and monitor chat at the same time.
Why we pre-record

Pre-recording isn't a compromise — for a virtual, multitrack event it's the professional standard. Here's what it gives you:

Zero tech risk

No dropped Wi-Fi, no screen-share freezes. Your session plays flawlessly, every time.

Present your best

Record when you're at your sharpest. Re-take anything you like before we lock it.

Free to engage

You're in the chat with the audience instead of wrestling with your slides.

How will my session be conducted in Zoom Events?

You won't be figuring any of this out alone — I'll be with you for the recording, guiding the session from start to finish, and I'll send you simple prep instructions beforehand so you know exactly what to have ready. On event day, your recording is scheduled into the platform and plays automatically at your slot while you join the live chat.

The Event & Your Materials
The event is multitrack — what does that mean for me?
ValuSignal 2026 runs several tracks in parallel across the two days, so attendees choose the sessions most relevant to them. For you, it means a focused, self-selected audience in your slot — people who chose your topic. Your session time is fixed within the schedule, which is exactly why simulive works so well: it plays reliably in its track no matter what's happening elsewhere in the event.
Where do I find slide/deck templates?
Branded deck templates and presentation backgrounds are available in the media kit inside the speaker portal. Please build your slides on these — it keeps every session visually consistent and on-brand, and it saves you from designing from scratch.
