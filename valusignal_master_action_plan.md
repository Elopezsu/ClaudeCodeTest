### PLAN B — "OWNED MEDIA MACHINE"
**The consistency-and-volume strategy**

**Core idea:** LinkedIn rewards accounts that post consistently, not sporadically. Eunice's personal account is secondary; the brand itself does the heavy lifting.

#### Channel structure

| Channel | Role | Frequency |
|---------|------|-----------|
| Company page | Primary brand voice — all content types | 5–7x / week |
| Eunice's personal | Occasional amplification of key announcements | 1–2x / week max |
| Cold email | Conversion — synced with LinkedIn campaign topics | Running |
| UAD Ready Map | Lead magnet — every 3rd company post links to it | Weekly reference |

#### Content system (weekly rhythm Deanne runs)

**Monday — Speaker Spotlight**
Format: Headshot + name + title + 1 quote from their bio or session description. Copy: "On September 18, [Name] will walk you through [topic]. Here's why it matters." CTA: Early bird link.

**Tuesday — Industry Insight**
Format: Text post, no image. One sharp observation about UAD 3.6, AI, or appraiser business trends. 150 words max. Ends with a question to drive comments. Example: *"The average residential appraiser does 85% of their work for lenders. That number is about to change. What are you doing about it?"*

**Wednesday — Session Preview**
Format: Carousel or document post (highest LinkedIn dwell time). Show the session title, 3 bullet takeaways the attendee will get, speaker photo. CTA: "Early bird ends June 1."

**Thursday — Behind the Scenes or Free Asset**
Alternate between: (a) build story post from the 103-idea list rewritten for company page voice, (b) UAD Ready Map link with a new angle ("Which state is the least UAD 3.6 ready? We mapped it.").

**Friday — Social Proof or Milestone**
Ticket sold milestone, speaker acceptance story, sponsor announcement, or attendee testimonial. These posts anchor the week with credibility.
